Goodbye superfluous features!

Exit dark/light theme

It's just gonna stay dark. AD literally has 'dark' in it. Nah but also I'm not good at designing stuff and this was an easy shortcut. Also goes with 'simplification of everything simplifiable' effort.

No more profile banners

Uncluttering! We know, banners were great to plaster naked bodies on your profile, but we thought it'd be better to give more space to videos. That's where the actual juicy stuff is.

Likes/Followers listings begone

It was a customizable setting before, now the default is: not your profile? can't see the list of followers or liked videos. Not your video? You can't see who liked it. Just the numbers. Some discretion is nice in porn!

Bbye comment replies

Same thing, simplify stuff, unclutter interface, etc, etc… Comments themselves are not used a lot but we still think they're important, but replies is a little overdoing it.

In app chat messaging is gone

We realized that it really isn't used much, and users would usually go straight to user's twitter page (listed on their profile) to DM them.

Enter: The new stuff!

Global search, super buffed

The global search used to do its own thing sometimes and it was never 100% clear what that thing was. So it's been reworked a bit, just to make it more efficient and reliable.

Tags can make sense, if we code 'em right

Tags up until now were a MESS. For real. The tagging system has been revamped so that matching can work reliably, and to avoid typos and stuff like that.

Hey Twitter, check out our video quality

Until a few months ago, your videos were reprocessed to a pretty disappointing quality, because it saved time and bandwidth and money. We then upped it a bit so it'd be at least better than Twitter. But you know what, we like to see all the pixels clearly when we're watching a shiny latex pup annihilate a fursuit, so we've boosted up the size and bitrate of our encoder even MORE.

Bad people shoooo

The report and banning systems until now worked, but like, at their bare minimum: You report someone, you never get the update that the action has been taken. We changed that. It's nice to know you've been helpful. Also bans have been revamped to be less 'whatever' or 'banned for 500 years'. Hopefully you'll never have to find out!
